Introduction

Here are some of the key achievements of the Bangladesh cricket team in 2023, along with some of the challenges. If you want, I can also pull together detailed stats, major matches, or comparisons with previous years.

  1. Best ever year in terms of number of wins

    • Bangladesh played 49 international matches in 2023 and won 24 of them — their highest tally in a calendar year.
    • Their win percentage rose significantly — from ~37% in 2022 to about 49% in 2023.
    • The Under-19 team won the Asia Cup in 2023.

  1. Test Match Success

    • Bangladesh won three out of four Test matches during the year
    • They achieved a huge Test win against Afghanistan by 546 runs, which is Bangladesh’s largest margin of victory over any opponent, and one of the biggest in Test history.
    • They also beat New Zealand at home (in Sylhet) by 150 runs — a strong result given New Zealand’s quality.
    • In one Test, Najmul Hossain Shanto scored centuries in both innings — making him the second Bangladeshi after Mominul Haque to do so.
